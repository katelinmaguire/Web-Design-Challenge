Latitude vs. Humidity

We do not see any linear trend in the humidity percentage data. According to the plot above we do see that there is a concentration of cities that shows humidity at 100%; this is true for cities in both the Northern and Southern hemispheres. There are also some cities at 60-80 degrees latitude that have a humidity percentage at or close to zero.
